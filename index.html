Welcome to Leap Band Project Pages.

Introduction

Leap Band introduce a new way that allows you to interacte with machine using your finger/hand gestures, users are able to play a variaty of instruments by only using their gestures, no physical contact required. What you need is a leap motion controller.

The project Leap Band also allows user to play different kinds of music with other players, you can compose your music and play them with your friends.

The project is also under the frame of UV NF28 and IA04 of the UTC.

Information & Support

If you want to know more about Leap Band, you can go to the official blog for project LeapBand at http://leapband.fr/blog or contact support@leapband.fr and we are happy to response all kind of question about the project.

If you want to know more, you can read our specifications sheet at Goole Docs (in French).
