Leapband

Playing music in a brand new way with your friends

Download this project as a .zip file Download this project as a tar.gz file

Play mode selection:

In this screen, you can choose either single mode or Mutiple mode, or you can quit from the game.

Instrument Selection:

In the view, you can choose your instrument by swiping and grabing your hand.

Room selection:

In this window, you can enter an existing room or you can create your own room.

 Waiting room:

In this screen, you can look all membres in your group, if you are the host, you can start the game at anytime.

But if you are not the host, you can only wait for start or quit the room.

 Game view:

In this view, you can play the music with your selected instrument.

You can also chage the background music as you wish.
